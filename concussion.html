Information

A concussion is a type of TBI, a traumatic brian injury, that is caused by a bump, blow, or jolt to the head or a hit to the body that cause the head and brain to move rapidly back and forth. Concussions can leave long lasting impacts on a persons brain icluding their memory and ability to focus.

Treatment

When someone has a concussion, it is very important that the rest, both physically and mentally. This means that activities are very limited until your symptoms, including headaches and dizziness, are lessened.
