Welcome to Health Helper

Our Purpose

We were inspired by a previous Girls Who Code final project called Pocket Doctor. However, we wanted to make our page more informative and be able to connect user with resources. We also wanted to make our website easier to manage so that anyone at any age could go in and use it.

About the Founders

Kelcey Lingat, Roshni Supur, Ali Shapiro, and Geila Vazquez are four teenage girls who learned to code through the Summer Immersion Program with Girls Who Code. Kelcey Lingat is 17 and is an incoming senior at Benjamin N. Cardozo High School. She wants to study Nursing at Hunter College. Roshni Surpur is 16 and an incoming junior at Choate Rosemary Hall. She wants to study Computer Science in Business for a role as product operations manager. Ali Shapiro is 16 and an incoming junior at John F. Kennedy High School in Bellmore, NY. She hopes to study Biology and Computer Science to find a way to merge the computer science and medical fields. Geila Vazquez is 17 and an incoming senior at The Academy of Finance and Enterprise. She wants to study Law and go to a four year college in the city.
