Information

Overview: Menstruation is one part of the menstrual cycle of a woman which includes the shedding of the uterin lining that occurs abiout once a month. The typical age for a girl to get her period is between age 10 and age 15. There are some signs that menstruation will start soon. Typically, a girl gets her period two years after her breasts start to grow and she may experience vaginal discharge fluid 6 months to a year prior to getting her first period.

During Your Period

There are many options for what you can do while on your period. One of these options is to wear a pad which you will change every 4 hours or so. Other people use tampons and change them every 4 to 6 hours. While on your period you may experience cramps in your abdomen which can be helped by taking Advil, Tylenol, or other medications similar to those.
