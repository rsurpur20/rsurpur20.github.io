Information

Overview: STD's are sexually transmitted diseases are typically passed from person to person through sexual contact. There are many types of STD's, some of the most common being HPV, Chlamydia, Gonorrhea, Syphylis, and Herpes.

HIV and AIDS

HIV and AIDS are two other common STD's. HIV is the gateway disease that leads to AIDS if not treated with anti-retroviral drugs. HIV is spread through blood, vaginal fluid, breast milk, and semen. HIV and AIDS can be prevented with by using a condom during sexual activity, never sharing needles, and abstinence, among other things.
